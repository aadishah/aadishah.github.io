This research is related to my doctoral research.

Research Objective: The research study is aimed at understanding working professionals. The study aims to understand the self-perceptions of individuals. It attempts to analyze how individuals relate to others. It also tries to articulate how individuals deal with various inconveniences and hurdles of their life. Through this it attempts to get a view of their adaptability. The research also aims to, in part, to understand an individual’s interactional patterns with their spouse. The purpose is to help working professionals and individuals to build healthy interactional patterns and to develop a robust interventional strategy through the knowledge attained from this research.
